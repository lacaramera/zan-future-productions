Rewind Studios is a fan-made entertainment project focused on recreating iconic concert experiences inside Roblox with a cinematic and immersive visual style.

Our goal is to pay tribute to memorable live shows through staging, lighting, atmosphere and creative production, made only for community enjoyment and without commercial purposes.

This project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the original artists, brands, labels or rights holders. All trademarks, music, images and related intellectual property belong to their respective owners.

We do not accept any kind of monetization, paid access, donations or profit related to these tribute experiences.

This experience is inspired by Soda Stereo, one of the most influential bands in Latin American rock history. Their music, visual identity and live performances continue to connect generations across countries and time.

Rewind Studios is creating this Roblox tribute as a fan-made, non-profit experience. We encourage every visitor to explore the band’s official platforms, listen to their music and support the original work.
